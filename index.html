Description


Celebrate the true meaning of Christmas with this exquisite Nativity Story Ornament Collection, carefully crafted from authentic olive wood sourced directly from Bethlehem, the sacred birthplace of Jesus. Each piece in this collection beautifully depicts a key moment from the Bible’s Nativity sequence, allowing you to journey through the miraculous events that unfolded over 2,000 years ago.

From Mary and Joseph’s journey to Bethlehem, the joyful announcement to the shepherds, the reverent visit of the Magi guided by the star, to the humble scene of Jesus in the manger – each ornament captures a piece of the story, transforming your Christmas tree into a display of faith and tradition. Every detail is meticulously carved by skilled artisans from Christian families in Bethlehem, bringing authenticity and craftsmanship to your holiday decorations.

What’s Included:

  • A full collection of twelve ornaments, each symbolizing a chapter in the Nativity story.
  • Unique shapes, including stars, bells, and ovals, that enhance the visual appeal of your tree or home decor.
  • Depictions of angels, the Holy Family, the shepherds, and the journey to Egypt, allowing you to relive each sacred event with every glance.

Why This Collection is Special?
These ornaments are not only beautiful Christmas decorations but also meaningful symbols of hope, love, and peace. As each piece is crafted from olive wood, the very same wood type mentioned throughout the Bible, you’re bringing a piece of the Holy Land into your home. This collection is an heirloom set that can be cherished for generations, reminding your family of the Christmas story and the true spirit of the holiday.

Add a touch of history, faith, and authenticity to your Christmas this year with a collection that honors the birth of Jesus and supports the artisans who live in His birthplace.
